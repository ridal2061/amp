What Are Google Ads Certificates covers

Google Analytics Individual Qualification

The Google Analytics Individual Qualification (IQ) is a demonstration of proficiency in Google Analytics that is available to any individual who has passed the Google Analytics IQ exam.

Google Ads Video Certification

The Video Advertising exam covers basic and advanced concepts, including best practices for creating, managing, measuring, and optimizing video advertising campaigns across YouTube and the web.

Google Ads Shopping Certification

The Shopping Advertising exam covers basic and advanced concepts, including creating a Merchant Center account and product data feed, and creating and managing Shopping campaigns

The Search Advertising exam covers basic and advanced concepts, including best practices for creating, managing, measuring, and optimizing search ad campaigns across the Search Network.

Google Ads Mobile Certification

The Mobile Advertising exam covers the basic and advanced concepts of mobile advertising, including ad formats, bidding and targeting, and campaign measurement and optimization.

Google Ads Display Certification

The Display Advertising exam covers advanced concepts and best practices for creating, managing, measuring, and optimizing Display campaigns.

What is an Adwords / Google Ads Consultant?

What is an Adwords / Google Ads Consultant? A Google AdWords consultant is a paid search expert on the Google search engine. His goal will be to generate qualified traffic on his client's website. It usually works for e-merchants or lead generators who have a complete web marketing strategy and want to set up or optimize Google Adwords campaigns. Its goal will be to achieve the best return on investment (ROI) for its customers.

He does not work directly for Google but has an official Google Partner certification as well as many years of experience in managing paid search campaigns.

We can also name it: Expert in sponsored links, Traffic Manager, Freelance Adwords, Google Adwords Agency 

What are his missions?

By working with a Freelance Adwords consultant he will be brought to:

- Analyze your sector, the potential of your business to generate profitable Adwords campaigns based on your current conversion rate.

-Set up your account and goals correctly by also checking that Google Analytics works well to get the set of statistics

-Determine an organization of campaigns, ad groups, and campaign types to set up

-Choose and propose the right keywords with the right targeting (exact keywords, regular expressions, broad queries ...)

-Setting up your campaigns by determining ROI at attet constant optimization of your ROI

-Write ads that make you want to click and add various extensions to boost the click rate of the ad.

-Perform a rigorous management of manual or automatic bidding according to your objectives and volume.

-Make regular reports to its customers to be in continuous optimization phase, especially by optimizing the quality level of keywords.

-Reduce acquisition costs and increase the conversion rate of your site for an equivalent budget.

-An Adwords Consultant (or PPC Expert) takes care of the audit of existing campaigns to the creation of new campaigns, up to tracking and analysis of results.
